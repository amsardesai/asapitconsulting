Services We Offer

ASAPIT Consulting specializes in providing support services to meet the needs of small and expanding industry. We can help streamline your operations to increase efficiency, and maximize profits. Our services include:

  • OS-DB Migration:
    We offer certified SAP OS/DB migration consultanting. Combined with ASAPIT's thought leadership in the SAP hosting industry, we now bring our unique perspective to migrations in and out of local and hosted scenarios, as well as migrations into virtualized environments.
  • Upgrade Planning, Testing, & Execution:
    We've performed dozens of production upgrades for ECC, BI, CRM, SRM, SCM & Solution Manager keeping an eye on reducing production downtimes.
  • Software Installation of SAP Products:
    We are certified installers of SAP products. We do it right the first time. We also provide SAP Technical Implementation cutover testing, and go-live support.
  • Performance Optimization and Tuning:
    We can pinpoint problems inside/outside of SAP which affect your system performance. With ASAPIT you get expertise in all areas needed to run a world class SAP system.
  • Solution Manager:
    We offer implementation and configuration for CTS+, ChaRM, Service Desk, Business Process Monitoring, and Root Cause Analysis scenarios.
  • Routine Administration:
    We've setup the daily checklist and been able to automate some of the processes which consume much of an admin's day. Although tedious it's critical to keep systems running healthy.
  • Background Processing:
    Keeping a watchful eye on the background system keeps your nightly processes running well and project members happy. We have many times tuned job schedules to make system output more in less time.
  • Remote Technical Support:
    Providing quick and thorough support to you developer and configuration teams is our speciality. We're there to help with the application of OSS notes, debug ABAP code to fix functional problems; and troubleshoot poorly written reports and interfaces.
  • CCMS Monitoring:
    Setup of CCMS alerting is more powerful than ever. ASAPIT supports email and paging alerts natively so you won't necessarily need to buy a huge monitoring package if you're only looking for alerting and not trend analysis. If you want to monitor your systems within SAP you need to talk to us.
  • Technical Documentation:
    We will help you document the processes and technology which runs your IT organization. We love to document and we are good at it. Not only is it great backup but it's excellent for training and support! Where other techies balk at documentation, we thrive. Contact us and we'll prove it.
  • Change Management:
    We are experts in SAP Change Management. Find out why the market leading change management tool company relies upon our expertise to lead their North American implementations.
  • Disaster Recovery:
    We will help to ensure your company is ready for the worst case scenario – your data center needs to be recreated at a remote location – by planning and performing necessary testing.
