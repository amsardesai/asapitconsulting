About ASAPIT Consulting Inc.

ASAPIT Consulting services was started in 2006 with the goal of providing support services for the growing industry demand. Our team has brought together our varied backgrounds in industry, process management, and experience in both large industry and small businesses. We strive to meet the needs of small and expanding business.

We have successfully completed various IT Projects with reputed consulting houses such as Hewlett Packard - EDS, KPMG Consulting, IBM, SAP Canada in different capacities.

Backed by a distinguished and rewarding career, the CEO of the company Mahesh Sardesai has an experience of more than 2 decades and has made a mark in multiple business work and geographical environments.
